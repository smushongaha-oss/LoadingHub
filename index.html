How Tolls Are Charged
● Toll fees are added on top of the base fare automatically
● The fare calculator shows a 🛂 Toll Line in the breakdown
● Fees depend on your vehicle class (light/kombi/truck)
● All amounts are in USD as set by ZINARA
● Drivers can use the 💬 Negotiate button to discuss who covers toll fees
● Tolls are tracked in the driver earnings dashboard
